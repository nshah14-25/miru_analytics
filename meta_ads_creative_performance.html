Creative strategy insights

Scale this

Stacks DPA carousel is your #1 performer

ALL-PRODUCTS_CAROUSEL_STACKS-DPA drove 7.23x ROAS at just $20 CPR in P1 — by far your most efficient buying. In P2 it split into two campaigns and still delivered 5.3x–6.6x. This format + product combination is your most scalable asset.

Scale this

Video surged in P2 — biggest format shift

Video went from 1.50x ROAS in P1 to 2.91x in P2. Daily Textures V3 video is your best non-DPA evergreen performer. The rebrand shoot videos (Homepage Teaser, Shoot Pics 2) also performed well at 2.19x and 1.72x with meaningful scale.

Pause or test

GIFs are collapsing in P2

GIF format dropped from 1.97x ROAS in P1 to just 0.91x in P2 — below break-even for most AOVs. "You Can't Have Only One" GIF was previously a solid performer. Don't invest new GIF creative budgets until you diagnose the drop.

New winner

Hoops & Gym Set emerged as a strong new creative

HOOP-HUGGIES Gym-Set-1A drove 2.42x ROAS with 21 purchases in P2 at $87 CPR. It's a new creative with meaningful spend ($1.8k) — a clear signal to build out more Hoops creative variations and test higher budgets.

Efficiency dropping

Titanium "New Standard" is losing steam

Your biggest P1 ad by spend ($21k, 225 purchases) dropped to 1.12x ROAS in P2. CPR jumped from $94 to $124. The new Titanium rebrand creative (TITANIUM-LDP_New-Standard_04-07) is also underperforming at 1.43x. Refresh the Titanium creative concept urgently.

Watch

Lab Grown Diamond spend doubled but ROAS fell

LGD spend grew from $4.1k to $10.6k in P2, but ROAS fell from 1.65x to 1.38x. Sparkle & Float videos are not converting well. Evergreen "3 Truths 1 Lie" video is holding at 1.85x but that's thin margin. LGD needs a creative rethink or tighter targeting.

For next batch

Mobius is showing early promise — build on it

The two Mobius static ads (NewCollection_04-07_01 and _02) hit 3.05x and 3.90x ROAS with low spend. Early signal only, but the product converts well. Prioritise video creative for Mobius in the next batch to test whether it scales.

For next batch

Secret Iteration and Daily Textures are your evergreen anchors

Secret Iteration 1B image and Daily Textures V3 video appear in both periods with consistent performance (2.5–2.8x ROAS). These are proven concepts — develop new iterations with seasonal or rebrand messaging rather than starting from scratch.
